Vegetarian Chilli Recipe

Description

An easy to make, delicious vegetarian chilli dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 finely chopped carrots
  • 2 finely chopped celery sticks
  • 2 finely chopped onions
  • 2 tablespoons of dried mixed herbs
  • 2 crushed garclic cloves
  • 1 sliced red pepper
  • 1 sliced green pepper
  • 2-3 tablespoons of chilli powder
  • 2 tablespoons of sweet smoked paprika
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato pur√©e
  • 400g canned drained red kidney beans
  • 400g cannned drained black beans
  • 2x 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 400ml vegetable stock
  • Cooked rice, grated cheddar and soured cream

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over low medium heat.
  2. Fry the carrots, celery, onions and mixed herbs for 10-12 mins, stir occasionally until soft but not golden.
  3. Stir the garlic and both peppers.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes until peppers soften.
  5. Sprinkle in chilli powder and paprika.
  6. Turn up the heat to medium, stir and cook for 1min.
  7. Pour in all of the beans, tomatoes and stock.
  8. Stir well and bring to boil.
  9. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook for 25-35 mins until beans are tender and stock is thick.
  10. Serve with rice, grated cheddar and sour cream.
