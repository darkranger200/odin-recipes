Chicken Curry

Description

An easy chicken curry recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of sunflower oil
  • 1 thinly sliced onion
  • 2 crushed garclic cloves
  • 1 thumb sized piece of grated ginger
  • 6 boneless and skinless chicken thighs
  • 3 tablespoons of medium spice paste (i.e tikka)
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 100g of Greek yogurt
  • 1 small bunch of chopped corriander leaves
  • 50g of ground almonds
  • naan breads or cooked basmati rice

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a flameproof casserole dish or large frying pan over medium heat
  2. Add the onion as well as a generous pinch of salt
  3. Fry until the onion has turned golden brown and sticky (about 10 minutes)
  4. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another minute
  5. Chop the chicken into 3cm pieces
  6. Add to the pan and fry for 5 mins
  7. Add 250mL of water
  8. Stir through the spice paste and tomatoes
  9. Bring to a boil, then lower to a simmer
  10. Cook on gentle heat, uncovered for 25-30 minutes
  11. Stir through the yogurt, corriander and ground almonds
  12. Season and serve with warm naan or fluffy basmati rice
